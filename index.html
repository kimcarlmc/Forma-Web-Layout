HOW IT WORKS

From concept to prototype in four steps

Forma removes the gap between ideation and execution. No handoff, no lost context, no separate tools — one continuous workflow for the whole team.

  1. Import or start fresh

    Bring in CAD files, glTF models, or begin with Forma's built-in primitive library.

  2. Design & prototype

    Apply materials, lighting, and interactions. Preview at any viewport — XR, mobile, or desktop.

  3. Collaborate in real-time

    Invite teammates with a link. Comment on geometry, leave annotations, and iterate together.

  4. Export & ship

    Publish to a live URL, export optimized WebGL, or hand off specs to engineering directly.
